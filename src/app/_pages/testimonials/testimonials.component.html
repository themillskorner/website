Chris Hollis

Coach Kevin Mills, Has been a mentor and father figure to me since I was about 16 years old. He was my Head Football Coach at Pasadena High School. Coach Kevin Mills helped push me to understand how to overcome adversity, and accept new challenges in life. Also how to get through tough circumstances without doubting myself.

Mills instilled in me that tough spirit of being a determined and motivated individual and not being susceptible to having the mentality of a victim and feeling like the world owes me something. Mills always told me that having an impact requires sacrifice and that loyalty is everything. Without coach Kevin Mills my life trajectory would have been very different due to my environment growing up as a young black man in my neighborhood.

Due to Coach mills mentor-ship of me, i continued my football and academic career in college at Mount San Antonio College. Where I graduated with an Associates Degree in Kinesiology and Wellness, and made the deans list for my academic achievements. From there i went on to Azusa Pacific University where i continued my football and academic career, and graduated with a Bachelors Degree in Sociology. While being a intern for a well known business organization called "Enactus", where i held the position off discussion facilitator. I am currently working in the area of community development in Los Angeles County. I serve as a Board Member as well for a non-profit organization called "ImPower Youth" at the age of twenty-six years old. Along with all of that i am continuing my education in pursuits of an M.B.A degree from University's such as, USC, Howard University, Georgetown, Chicago Booth School of Business, and a few more. I say all this to conclude that without Coach Kevin Mills in my life none of this would be possible at all. he instilled in me the tools at a critical age in my life to succeed and overcome everything that life has thrown at me.
