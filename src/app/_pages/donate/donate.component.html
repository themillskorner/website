The impact of your gift

Your tax-deductible gift to our 501c3 non-profit, themillskorner will ensure all mentees/youths have a caring adult mentor to help them secure academic success, confidence to be the best versions of themselves, and be college and career ready when they graduate from high school. Our mentees need your support:

  • 75% - 100% of our students live at or below the Federal poverty line.
  • Each year themillskorner serves approximately 1,500 at-risk students attending public schools in low-income communities.
  • Students build persistent academic, career, college, and life goals with mentor guidance and support.

With your support more mentees will have access to mentors to guide them through themillskorner curricular activities focusing on academic success, career exploration and post-secondary planning. Themillskorner mentors help mentees increase their social capital by developing networks of supportive adults to help them reach their full potential.
